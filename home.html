Jack's Equipment Company

Jack's Equipment Company is a small, family-owned business located just outside of Greenfield Ohio. Jack Hutchinson has been the owner since 2015. Jack's goal has been to give Greenfield and South Central/Southeast Ohio a reliable place to buy and service construction, forestry, and lawn equipment. Jack's Equipment Company is also the only supplier of Bad Boy Mowers, Accessories, and Implements such as brush cutters and land graders in and around South Central Ohio and is proud if it! Jack's Equipment Company is a certified dealer of many engine types, small equipment such as Echo Certified Trimmers and Chainsaws, and more! Jack's Equipment Company also services and repairs Bad Boy equipment as well as large equipment such as (but not limited to) Skidders, Excavators, Dozers, and Backhoes. There are many more items located in our storefront. If you have any questions on products we may sell or services we may provide, please visit our Products pages or Contact Us!
